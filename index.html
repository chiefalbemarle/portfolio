Sample Healthcare Sales Deliverable

In this case study, I used a stock, provided dataset to produce a sample executive sales summary. I was able to provide all required/requested metrics and create a few additional forward looking measures (# Days, $/Day) that weren't specifically required. In this first version I've kept the approach simple, focusing on resolving spcecific user requested visuals. In a WIP I have taken more of a modeling approach and will provide additional capabilities through aggregates and relational modeling.
